Spock Beats Rock!

Rules

This game is a spin on the classic game of Rock Paper Scissors, whoich adds two more choices to the player.
The rules are simple (hover over image to enlarge):


Rock crushes Scissors

Scissors cut Paper

Paper covers Rock

Rock crushes Lizard

Spock vaporises Rock

Spock smashes Scissors

Paper disproves Spock

Lizard posions Spock

Lizard eats Paper

Scissors decapitate Lizard
